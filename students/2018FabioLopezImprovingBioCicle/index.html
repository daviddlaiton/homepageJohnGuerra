Improving BioCicle

Adaptation of the visualization tool for bioinformatics analysis

Undergrad project by Fabio Andrés López Corredor working under the supervision of John Alexis Guerra Gómez



Github Demo Poster Thesis

Objective

Revisit the BioCicle project (implemented by Meili Vanegas), allow an easier portability and availability online and adapt its setting to the new requirements from the users (bioinformaticians).

Problem Statement


Deliverables

  • Visualization available online, able to summarize and compare taxonomic reports.
  • Results and analysis of interaction from users with BioCicle.
